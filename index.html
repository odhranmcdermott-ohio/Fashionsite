About the Brand

L’Éclaireur des Cités means “The Scout of the Cities.” The brand mixes luxury streetwear with fall textures and bone-inspired design. Each piece is made to feel mysterious, sharp, and wearable in cold city weather.

The Collection

Mood & Inspiration

Fallen leaves. Cold streets. Bone structure. Concrete walls. Dark layering. The collection is built around the feeling of moving through the city in autumn.
