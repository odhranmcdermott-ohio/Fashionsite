Bag Piece

Relic Carrier

The Relic Carrier is the bag piece of the collection. It adds utility to the brand while still matching the fall and bone-inspired theme. The name suggests carrying something old, meaningful, or discovered in the city.

Design Concept

The bag would have a structured shape with straps, pockets, and bone-toned details. It could include abstract skeletal graphics or branch-like lines that connect back to the background artwork.

Materials & Colors

The bag would use dark leather, canvas, or heavy fabric. The color palette would include black, dark brown, ivory, and burnt orange accents.

Purpose in the Collection

This piece represents movement and survival in the city. It makes the collection feel more complete because it adds function, not just clothing.
