About the Class

The Story Behind the Collection

L’Éclaireur des Cités was created as part of a fashion design class project focused on building a complete brand identity and collection. Our group wanted to make something that felt different from regular fall fashion, so we combined autumn colors with bone-inspired imagery, dark urban style, and hand-drawn visual ideas.

The name L’Éclaireur des Cités means “The Scout of the Cities.” The brand is based on the idea of moving through the city during fall, surrounded by cold weather, shadows, concrete, fallen leaves, and quiet streets. The clothing is meant to feel mysterious, sharp, and expressive.

Class Project Goals

In this project, we are learning how fashion brands are created from the ground up. Instead of only designing clothes, we are thinking about the full identity of a brand: the name, theme, colors, mood, target audience, product pieces, and website presentation.

Our Theme

Our main theme is fall and bones. Fall connects to change, decay, nature, and colder weather. Bones connect to structure, identity, strength, and the hidden form underneath the body. Together, these ideas help create a collection that feels both natural and urban.

The Four Pieces

Boneframe Top

The top piece is inspired by skeletal structure and layering. It represents the main body of the collection and connects the bone theme directly to wearable streetwear.

Veil of the City

The balaclava piece adds mystery and protection. It fits the cold-weather fall theme while also giving the brand a darker city-inspired look.

Relic Carrier

The bag piece brings utility into the collection. It is designed to feel like something carried through the city, mixing function with the bone and autumn aesthetic.

Marrow Scarf

The scarf piece completes the fall look. It adds warmth, texture, and movement while connecting to the idea of bones, marrow, and layered design.

Visual Inspiration

Our visual inspiration includes skeleton trees, fallen leaves, rough textures, dark streets, old walls, abstract artwork, bones, and autumn colors like burnt orange, brown, ivory, black, and deep red. The background artwork on our site helps show this mood because it mixes bones, branches, words, and fall colors into one image.

What We Learned

Through this class project, we learned that fashion is not just about making clothes. It is also about storytelling. A strong brand needs a clear theme, a specific mood, and a reason behind each design choice. Every piece in our collection connects back to the same idea: autumn, bone, structure, and the city.
