Scarf Piece

Marrow Scarf

The Marrow Scarf is the softest and most layered piece in the collection. It connects directly to the fall theme because scarves are associated with colder weather, warmth, and seasonal layering.

Design Concept

The scarf would be long and textured, with distressed edges or woven patterns inspired by bones, branches, and decay. It would be dramatic enough to stand out but still wearable.

Materials & Colors

The piece would use warm fabric like wool, knit, or a soft woven material. The colors would include ivory, ash black, brown, and dark orange.

Purpose in the Collection

This piece represents warmth and texture. It completes the collection by adding movement, softness, and a clear fall feeling.
