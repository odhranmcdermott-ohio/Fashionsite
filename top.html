Top Piece

Boneframe Top

The Boneframe Top is the central clothing piece in the collection. It is inspired by the structure of bones and the way a skeleton creates the hidden frame of the body. The design connects to the brand’s fall theme through layering, darker tones, and a rough urban mood.

Design Concept

This piece would use bone-like lines across the chest, shoulders, and arms to create the feeling of a visible frame. The goal is not to make it look like a costume, but to turn skeletal structure into a high-fashion streetwear design.

Materials & Colors

The top would use dark fabric as the base, with ivory or bone-colored stitching, prints, or panels. Burnt orange and deep brown details could connect it to the autumn theme.

Purpose in the Collection

This piece represents structure. It shows how the body, the city, and the season all have hidden frameworks underneath them.
