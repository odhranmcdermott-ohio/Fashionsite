Balaclava Piece

Veil of the City

The Veil of the City is a balaclava piece designed around mystery, protection, and cold-weather fashion. It connects to the idea of moving through the city in fall while staying hidden, warm, and visually sharp.

Design Concept

The balaclava would include subtle bone-inspired stitching around the face opening and head shape. It would feel dark and minimal, but still clearly connected to the skeleton theme.

Materials & Colors

The piece would use soft black knit fabric with ivory stitching or small embroidered bone details. The fabric should feel warm, practical, and wearable.

Purpose in the Collection

This piece represents identity and mystery. It covers the face while still creating a strong visual statement, matching the brand’s darker city mood.
